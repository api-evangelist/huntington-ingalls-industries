Founded in 1886, HII’s Newport News Shipbuilding division has a storied legacy of advancing shipbuilding for the United States and making meaningful contributions to the community. NEWPORT NEWS — As the nation marks the 250th anniversary of American independence, the Hampton Roads region is reflecting on one of its most enduring contributions to the country — a shipbuilding legacy stretching back nearly 140 years.
